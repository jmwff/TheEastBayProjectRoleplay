Rules & Regulations

General Conduct

Metagaming, powergaming, and combat logging are not tolerated. Roleplay must stay in-character at all times while on duty or in an active scene. Breaking character to argue out-of-character in the middle of a scene ("breaking immersion") can result in a warning or removal from the scene.

Fail RP

Actions must have realistic in-character justification. Random deathmatching, unrealistic vehicle stunts used to escape roleplay, and refusing to comply with clearly established scene rules all fall under Fail RP and are subject to staff action.

Media SOP

Media department members must credit The East Bay Project in any published content, avoid streaming sensitive staff-only channels, and follow the same in-character conduct rules as any other member while filming.
