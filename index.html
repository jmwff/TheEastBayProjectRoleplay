Welcome to The East Bay Project Roleplay!

The East Bay Project is a FiveM community founded with a mission to provide a unique roleplaying experience in a family environment. Our goal is to provide you with a supportive and loyal community where your voice will be heard and your efforts appreciated. With numerous departments and subdivisions to choose from, with top-notch assets and scripts, we aim to offer a truly one-of-a-kind roleplaying experience. We believe that these aspects are what sets us apart and contributes to our thriving community, where long-lasting bonds are formed and every member is respected and cared for by the administration, staff, and peers. We're always here to help you make the most of your experience at The East Bay Project, and we can't wait to welcome you into our family.

Want to join The East Bay Project Roleplay?

Great! We'd love to have you here. We have plenty of departments for you to join. At the top of the website you will see "Open Applications" make sure you click on that then select "Recruit Application" that will redirect you to our server application. Please read all of The East Bay Project's rules and regulations and community requirements before applying, thanks!

Don't have a PC but still want to be a part of The East Bay Project, the Communications Department is the place for you! The San Andreas Communications Department allows individuals who don't have a strong enough PC to join us in-game still to patrol and make a difference in the community. They're absolutely crucial too! Communications provides the highest level of organization for patrol channels. Here at The East Bay Project, nobody is excluded. We want to join our community! If you have any questions at all, you can join our Fan Discord under "Resources" tab, or review the FAQ under "Resources" tab as well.

Have a question but unsure where to ask it?

No worries! We have a public Discord which you can join by clicking the red text right HERE and inside the Discord we have multiple support channels and a ticket system with staff ready to assist you! We can answer any and all of your questions you might have about the server, and you can find out more about The East Bay Project in this Discord!

Community Age Requirement

The East Bay Project is determined to provide an entertaining, respectful, and mature environment for all of our members. To ensure that those goals are met, we have set our community age limit to 16 years or older.
